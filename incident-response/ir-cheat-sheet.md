A simplified reference for SOC analysts during active incidents.

---

## Initial Triage
- Identify IOC type (IP / URL / Hash / Email)
- Check severity & confidence
- Look for active exploitation

---

## Investigation
- Correlate across IP, domain, hash, and email
- Identify lateral movement indicators
- Map to MITRE ATT&CK techniques

---

## Containment
- Block IPs / Domains / Hashes
- Quarantine affected hosts
- Reset compromised credentials

---

## Eradication & Recovery
- Remove persistence mechanisms
- Patch exploited vulnerabilities
- Restore from clean backups

---

## Post-Incident
- Document findings
- Improve detection rules
- Update threat intelligence
